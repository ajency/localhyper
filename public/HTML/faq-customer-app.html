FAQs

Why do I have to 'Make request'?

On Make request, your request is sent to all local sellers, and the sellers will send you their prices (offers) for the requested product.

Why do I have to enter my delivery address on Make request ?

Sellers will respond to your request with their price and delivery time.Hence for the sellers to determine the delivery time, you have to enter the delivery address

When shall I start receiving offers from the sellers?

Once the request is made your request stands valid for 24 hours. Your are expected to receive offers anytime before the request expires.However the receiving offers is solely dependent on availability of sellers

Can I cancel my request?

Yes you can cancel your request until you accept an offer, once offer is accepted, you will not have the option to Cancel your request

What happens when I accept an offer?

When the offer is accepted, the seller will be notified about the same and seller’s contact and address will be displayed to you. You can then contact the seller.

What can I do if I have any question to sellers?

On Make request, there is a option to enter your comments, so you can enter your questions as comments, to which sellers will provide answers when they make their offers.

How do I know that my order is confirmed?

Once you accept your offer,the status of the offer changes to pending delivery,You will subsequently receive a call from the seller to confirm the same and begins processing your order.

What is the payment method I have to use?

Cash on delivery,cheque,netbanking or anything that the seller accepts.This decision will be taken by the seller once you accept and confirm the offer.

Can I Cancel my request after accepting an offer?

No. You can’t do this.

Can I request for more than one product at a time?

Yes very well. There is no limit on the number of requests you can make, However request for every product is valid only for 24 hours.

What do I do once my request expires?

You can make a new request now, and view the previous made request under ’ My requests‘.

Where can I check all my requests?

My requests page. Your request details and the status of the requests can be seen here.
